Day 3

Something massive is rumbling beneath the foundation of EDC. A legion of warriors approaches, set to storm this sacred, space-age temple of boom. Flags raised high, they travel beneath the banners of dubstep, trap, drum and bass, and other tribes loyal to the most primal of frequencies. In this circle of fire and air, their energy is unified and their purpose is true. They are not slaves to the rhythm; they are set free by its power.
